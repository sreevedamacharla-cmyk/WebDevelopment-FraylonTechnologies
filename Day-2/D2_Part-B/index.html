Headings in HTML

Headings in HTML denote the importance and structure of content. Importance of headings decrease from h1 to h6.

h4 is used for Deeper levels of organization

This is Heading 5

h5 is used for Smaller Subdivisions

This is Heading 6

Text Formatting

< Strong > is used to indicate the strong importance of a text.

< b > tag makes text Bold without adding any importance to it.

< em > tag is used for Emphasis. It displays the text in italic.

< i > tag is used to just display a text in Italic without adding any emphasis.

< mark > is used for Highlighting text.

< sub > is used to display any Subscript text

< sup > is used to display any Superscript text..

< small > represents small and less important text
